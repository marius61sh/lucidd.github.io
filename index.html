Overview

About This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Lucidd mobile application, developed by Licvora Labs ("we", "us", or "our").

This app is provided as a Freemium service and is intended for use "AS IS". By using the Application, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Important

Not a Medical App

This application does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. It is intended for general wellness and informational purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical or mental health concerns.

Account

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for authentication and account recovery
  • Display name — used to personalize your in-app experience

This data is stored securely via Firebase Authentication and is never sold to third parties.

Sensitive Data

Mental Health & Wellness Data

To deliver its core features, Lucidd collects information that may be considered sensitive personal data:

  • Mood check-ins — daily emotional state and selected activities
  • Sleep logs — hours slept and sleep quality ratings
  • Journal entries — free-text personal writing stored privately
  • AI conversation history — messages exchanged with the in-app AI assistant
  • Challenge & article progress — daily task completion and streaks

This data is processed solely to provide and improve the Application's features. It is stored in your private account and is not shared with other users or used for advertising.

The legal basis for processing this data is your explicit consent, given when you create an account and use the app's features.

Device & Usage

Automatically Collected Data

We may collect the following automatically when you use the Application:

  • Device information (e.g. operating system, device model)
  • Usage data (features used, time spent in app)
  • IP address (collected by Firebase infrastructure)

We do not collect precise location data.

Some data is cached locally on your device using AsyncStorage to allow the app to work offline and load faster. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted unless you are signed in.

AI Features

Artificial Intelligence

The Application uses AI to power the chat assistant and provide personalized insights. When you interact with the AI feature, your messages are sent to OpenAI for processing.

OpenAI may process and temporarily store these messages in accordance with their own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing OpenAI's Privacy Policy for full details.

We do not use AI to make automated decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects on users.

Third Parties

Third-Party Services

The Application integrates the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy governing how it handles your data:

  • Firebase Authentication — account management
  • Firebase Firestore — cloud storage of your app data
  • Firebase Analytics — anonymous usage analytics
  • Google Play Services — Android platform services
  • OpenAI — AI assistant processing
  • Subscription provider — manages in-app purchases and subscriptions
Subscriptions

In-App Purchases

Lucidd offers a Freemium model with optional paid subscriptions. Payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play (Android). We do not collect or store your payment card information.

Purchase history and subscription status may be shared with our subscription management provider to verify your access level within the app.

Portability

Data Export

You can export a copy of your personal data — including check-in history, journal entries, and AI analyses — directly from the app via Settings → Export my data. The export is generated as a PDF file saved to your device.

Account Deletion

Delete Your Account

You can request full deletion of your account and all associated data (mood history, journal entries, sleep logs, AI conversations, and account credentials) at any time by contacting us at getlucidd.app@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete my account".

We will process your request within 14 days and confirm deletion by email.

Retention

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed from our systems within 14 days.

Some anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics purposes and cannot be linked back to you.

Age Restrictions

Children's Privacy

This Application is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the European Union, where a higher age threshold applies for digital consent).

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Security

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit using industry-standard protocols.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
